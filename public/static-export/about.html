About BookNest

Connecting book lovers across India to share, swap, and discover amazing reads.

Our Story

BookNest was born from a simple observation: we all have books we've loved, read, and are ready to pass on, while there are countless other books we're eager to discover. Instead of letting these treasures gather dust on shelves, why not connect readers and give every book a new adventure?

Founded in 2024, we've grown into a vibrant community of thousands of book lovers who share our passion for reading and sustainable living. Whether you're looking for rare finds, textbooks, or the latest bestsellers, BookNest is your gateway to an endless library.

What We Believe

Love of Reading

We believe books are meant to be shared and enjoyed by as many people as possible.

Community First

Building connections between book lovers in neighborhoods across India.

Sustainability

Give your books a second life and reduce waste by swapping instead of buying new.

Accessibility

Making great books accessible to everyone, regardless of budget.
